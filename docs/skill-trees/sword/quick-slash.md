## Design Notes

- **Stage 1-2:** Basic improvements, combo starters, utility
- **Stage 3:** Specialization begins (DoT, control, speed)
- **Stage 4:** Powerful combat skills, AoE, executions
- **Stage 5:** Legendary/ultimate abilities, game-changing effects

Each child skill can further evolve into its own tree with 4 more children per stage.
